ABOUT ME

Jeong Hyun Lee — 이정현 — 李政炫

Photo of Me

I am a senior at Ithaca Senior High School, located in Ithaca, NY. I was born in Seoul, South Korea and moved to the US the first time in 2004. Then in 2005, I returned to Korea, only to come back after 4 years in 2009. There, I graduated from Northeast Elementary School and attended DeWitt Middle School. After 3 years in the US, I transfered to Eonju Middle School back in Korea. Lastly, my final move so far was in 2014, back to Ithaca, to Ithaca High School. For all my stays in the US, I have lived in Ithaca, NY.

Fun facts about me

  • Having parents who are both professors, I have followed them to visit 19 different countries around the world.
  • I have only a elementary school diploma.
